Every Altar Kit includes:

• A pop up altar

• “Calaveritas” (a ceramic skull)

• “Cempazúchitl flowers”, the Mexican flowers that are an important part of every traditional altar of Mexico

• “Xolo” (pronounced cholo) and “Alebrije”, spirits of dog and incredible animals that guide loved ones on their after-life journeys

• A glass to quench the departed's thirst on their journey

Designed by Ana Beatriz. She is originally from Veracruz, Mexico. She grew up in Mexico City and later lived for more than 30 years in Cancún.
